ISSUE 1 - The Cailleach


The Cailleach or "Veiled one", was a Celtic goddess who ruled over the winds and winter.
Appearing as an old, veiled woman, she was both creator and destroyer,
and served as a patron of animals, particularly wolves.

By Gregory Wright
